What is "Big Brother"? 🤔

Imagine you have 9 mangoes 🥭 but you want to share with your friend who has 10 apples 🍎.

"Big Brother" means — when your number is TOO BIG to add directly on the abacus, you ask your Big Brother (the next column) to help you! 🙌

Big Brother gives you 10 and you give back the extra ones. That's the trick! ✨

🔟 The 10-Friends Table

These two numbers are BEST FRIENDS — they always add up to 10! 🤝
When you see one, the other is its friend!
9 + 1 = 10  ·  8 + 2 = 10  ·  7 + 3 = 10 and so on!

🌈 Story Examples for Kids!
🍭

Candy Story (+9)

Priya has 5 candies 🍭.
Mama gives her 9 more!

The abacus is too full for 9!
So we use Big Brother 👫:
Add 10, Remove 1
5 + 10 = 15 → 15 − 1 = 14!

🎉 Answer = 14 candies!
🐣

Chicks Story (−8)

Raju has 12 baby chicks 🐣.
8 chicks flew away!

Not enough to remove 8!
Ask Big Brother 👫:
Remove 10, Add back 2
12 − 10 = 2 → 2 + 2 = 4!

🎉 Answer = 4 chicks!
🌸

Flower Story (+7)

Anu picked 6 flowers 🌸.
She picks 7 more!

Use Big Brother 👫:
Add 10, Remove 3
6 + 10 = 16 → 16 − 3 = 13!

🎉 Answer = 13 flowers!
🍪

Cookie Story (+90)

The bakery made 30 cookies 🍪.
They made 90 more!

Big Brother gives 100 👫:
Add 100, Remove 10
30 + 100 = 130 → 130 − 10 = 120!

🎉 Answer = 120 cookies!
⭐ Easy Tips to Remember!
🤝

Friends of 10

Every number has a friend! 1+9, 2+8, 3+7, 4+6, 5+5. They always make 10 together!

➕

Adding Big Numbers

Too big to add? Ask Big Brother! Add 10, then remove the friend number.

➖

Taking Away Big Numbers

Too big to remove? Ask Big Brother! Remove 10, then add back the friend number.

💯

100s Work the Same!

For tens (10, 20, 30...) it's the same trick but with 100 instead of 10!

🎵

Sing It!

Make a song: "9's friend is 1, 8's friend is 2..." Singing helps remember!

🖐

Use Your Fingers!

Hold up 10 fingers, fold down the number you want to remove. What's left = the friend!
